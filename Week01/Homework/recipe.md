# Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 pinch of salt
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. **Sift together** all *dry ingredients*. (flour, baking powder, and salt)
2. In a separate bowl, **cream the butter and sugar** until *smooth and creamy*.
3. **Add the egg** and mix until *fully incorporated*.
4. **Add the vanilla extract** and mix well
5. **Gradually add the dry ingredients** to the wet mixture until a *soft dough* forms.
6. **Fold in the chocolate chips** until *evenly distributed*.
7. **Let the dough rest** for *15–20 minutes*.
8. **Roll out the dough, cut into shapes**, and place them on a *parchment-lined baking tray*.
9. **Preheat the oven** to *180C (350°F)*.
10. **Bake** for *10–15 minutes*, or until *lightly golden*.
